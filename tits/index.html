Soyama Translation Services

Soyama Translations is an independent vendor that provides professional translation services located in the Eastern Cape of South Africa. You can find us in New Brighton Gqeberha. We offer timely and high quality translation services of any content from the English source to the targeted Xhosa language.

Please note we are only able to provide translation services from English to Xhosa.We are in the process of extending our services to other South African official languages. Please contact us with any questions you may have.
